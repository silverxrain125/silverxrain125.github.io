Truck&

Logistics/Transportation Management Platform

Previous Next

Truck& is a bidding platform that connects logistics companies and independent truck drivers. Once the company posts the loads that need transportation method, the truck drivers bid on the process: the lowest bid wins within a set timeframe. There are different levels of login system that distinguishes companies, truck drivers, and webmaster. The website provides necessary load-information such as distance required, expected weather forecast, and google-map to help the truck drivers making appropriate bidding price.

  • Date: March 2018
  • Technical skills used: Ruby on Rails, Bootstrap, Google API, Weather API, Balsamiq
  • Contribution: Concept Inception, UX Research, Create wireframes, Project management, UX Design, Back-end development (bidding system), Front-end development (bootstrap, email-subscription), Graphic Design

Airbnb for commercial real estate

Conscious

Click for mockup

Solution to Fast Fashion Waste

Previous Next

Conscious is a non-profit with the mission of increasing awareness about the environmental impact of cloth and motivate taking action among young children. ​​It is our mission to engage young children in reducing the environmental impact of clothing by educating them and motivating them to take simple steps. Altogether, these actions will make a significant impact.

Ideation process: Our Team's anchor purpose was to reduce the social and environmental impact of fast fashion through reuse and repurpose the waste. We applied design thinking framework and pivot multiple times to find the meaningful "How Might We" question. The industry research, interviews with stakeholders and end-users, and market research revealed that there is a lack of education on fashion waste and its environmental impact.

  • Contribution: Concept Inception, UX Research, Design Thinking, Agile Methodology, market research, competitive analysis, Internal process management, Powerpoint presentation, Graphic Design
